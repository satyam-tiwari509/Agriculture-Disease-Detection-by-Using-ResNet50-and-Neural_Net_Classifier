About PlantVillage Dataset

In order to ease plant disease diagnosis, the PlantVillage project began collecting and later opensourced images of crops for both healthy and diseased plant. The PlantVillage dataset contains images of leaves corresponding to 14 crop species: Apple, Blueberry, Cherry, Grape, Orange, Peach, Bell Pepper, Potato, Raspberry, Soybean, Squash, Strawberry, and Tomato.
